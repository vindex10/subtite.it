Subtite.it

Subtite by Scribefellas is the first community-driven service for transcribing educational content.

It allows motivated learners to make knowledge available beyond the existing language barriers.

How to get started?

  • 1. Install add-on

    Find here detailed instalation instructions for your favourite browser.

  • 2. Watch videos

    Our plugin is fully compatible with the usual YouTube video player. You just wath videos as you usually do.

  • 3. Contribute to subtitles

    Follow a kind suggestion to transcribe a few sentences from the video into your native language.
