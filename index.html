Make knowledge and entertainment available beyond the existing language barriers!
Developed by Scribefellas,
We are the first community-driven service for transcribing video content. Find more info in the Slides and join the crowd!

  • 1. Install add-on

    First download the app.

  • 2. Watch videos

    Our plugin is fully compatible with the usual YouTube video player. You just watch videos as you usually do.

  • 3. Contribute to subtitles

    Follow a kind suggestion to transcribe a few sentences from the video into your native language.

Why should you contribute?

  • Inspiration

    During the global lockdown, there’s a growing demand for online media content. This includes online courses, news stories, as well as recreational content such as youtube blogs and streaming services. One of the most crucial obstacle to access worldwide, is based on language.

    We think that having access to the world-leading online courses and the best TV shows should not depend on the language you speak. Subtite facilitates this crucial need.

  • Easiness

    The service provides you with a two-fold way to contribute into lifting the language barriers, by proposing your subtitles for the video or assessing the existing subtitles. In both cases, we're talking about a single line of subtitles. Once you completed or assessed that single line, your job is done!

    The workflow is easy and does not require any special efforts from you: you just watch educational videos as you usually do, and at the end of the video rate 5-second portion of subtitles or make your variant of subtitles for 5-second piece.

  • Community

    Users of our service is the community of people, fighting against language barriers and promoting worldwide access to information. By making consistent contributions, you become an important part of the YouTube community!

    By extraordinary contributions, you might become a member of the Leaders Board. The members of the Leaders Board are regularly invited onto our special tranings and webinars, and facilitate the growth and development of our community.
